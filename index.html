About Whisker Haven

Founded in 2015, Whisker Haven is a family-owned cat shop dedicated to providing the highest quality cats and accessories. We work with ethical breeders and prioritize the health and happiness of all our feline friends.

Our mission is to connect loving families with their perfect feline companions while offering premium products that enhance the lives of both cats and their humans.
